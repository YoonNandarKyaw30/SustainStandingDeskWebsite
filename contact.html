Frequency Asked Questions

Why should I use a standing desk?

A standing desk helps improve posture, reduces back pain, and promotes better circulation. It can also increase energy levels and productivity.

How long should I stand at my standing desk?

Experts recommend alternating between sitting and standing every 30 to 60 minutes to reduce strain and improve circulation.

Are standing desks good for back pain?

Yes! Standing desks can help alleviate lower back pain by promoting better posture and reducing prolonged sitting

What is the difference between a manual and an electric standing desk?

Manual desks use a crank or lever to adjust height. Electric desks adjust height with the push of a button for convenience.

Are standing desks height adjustable?

Yes! Most standing desks, including manual and electric models, allow you to adjust the height for a comfortable sitting or standing position.
